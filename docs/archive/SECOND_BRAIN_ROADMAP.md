## What Is a "Second Brain"?

A second brain is an external system that:
- **Captures** information from various sources
- **Organizes** it intelligently
- **Connects** related concepts across domains
- **Retrieves** relevant context proactively
- **Generates** insights and suggestions

Unlike a static knowledge base, a second brain is *active* - it thinks with you, not just for you.
